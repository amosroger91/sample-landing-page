About Antlers Manor Nursing Center

Antlers Manor Nursing Center is dedicated to providing exceptional, compassionate care for older adults. We offer a supportive environment where families can trust their loved ones will receive reliable, comprehensive, and high-quality services tailored to their unique needs, especially those managing chronic conditions. Our primary goal is to foster a community that nurtures the physical, social, and spiritual well-being of each resident. We are committed to preserving the dignity of every individual, empowering them with choices that promote the greatest possible independence and autonomy in their daily lives.
